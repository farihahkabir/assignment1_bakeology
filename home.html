In 2012, I first started selling my homemade chocolate chip cookies and cupcakes to my friends and family. After receiving wonderful feedback, I made a Facebook page and started selling to other people. As my success grew, I started selling more baked goods and soon I started my company, Baketopia.

All of my desserts are made fresh and by hand. We pride ourselves for using the freshest and best quality ingredients. We are slowly growing and continuously adding new items to the menu!

Without the support of our amazing customers, we would not be where we are now. For now, we only take orders online. We hope to soon open our own bakery in Dhaka.
